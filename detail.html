  3. (Optional) Talk to Bki Tbujhja in Lower Jeuno again. She mentions another set of Song Runes in Valkurm Dunes.
  4. Head to the Secret Beach at far west of Valkurm Dunes (C-6). Drop out onto the hidden beach and head south east to the bottom right of (B-7).
  5. Click on the Song Rune for another cutscene to complete the quest.
